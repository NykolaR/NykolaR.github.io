Who is this man?

Hi, this is Nykola, the sole developer of Darkness Reclamation. I am a software engineering student and indie game developer.

My main goal in starting this humble business is to make games that don't just play fundamental genres, but to attempt redefine them entirely. Part of this goal is achieved by rethinking the way games recieve input and deliver output, both in terms of visuals and audio cues. With unique approaches to old genres, new gameplay can be created, making a fresh experience for fans of them.

My first project, Darness Reclamation, is taking the Metroidvania genre and redefining the way the player interacts and understands their surroundings, not only through visual signifigance, but also dynamically through musical changes while exploring.
