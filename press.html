DESCRIPTION

Darkness Reclamation is an action platformer title, with inspiration drawn from the asthetics and exploration of Metroid, with the fast platforming of titles like Super Meat Boy. Featuring precise, flexible random generation, Darkness Reclamation provides players with a different experience every play through.

FEATURES

Randomly Generated:

  • Maps: Different map layouts generated every save game.
  • Areas: In each 40x40 map, unique rooms are created.
  • Weapons: Different weapons to collect every save game.
  • Abilities: New movement options for the player to navigate the world
  • Enemies: Randomized intelligence, with enemies that range from timid and passive, to aggressive and outgoing.
  • Enemy Weapons: Randomized weapons, influencing how they act.
  • Music: Every area has randomized music, with scales and tempos which relate to its characteristics.

Gameplay

  • Fast paced platforming, with sprinting and wall jumps.
  • Multiple weapon types, allowing for ranged combat or run and gun gameplay!
  • Randomly generated enemies adapted to randomly generated areas, creating unique enemy encounters.
  • Randomly generated bosses adapted to randomly generated boss areas, creating unlimited boss potential!
